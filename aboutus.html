Who Are We?

Pink Floyd was formed in London in 1965 we started out in rythm and blues but quickly reached international acclaim with progressive and psychedelic music. Lyrics that are thought provoking and some of the best quitar solo's in the world.

1965

Pink Floyd is formed, the first band members were Bob Klose and Roger Waters who both played guitar. Nick Mason on drums, Rick Wright on keyboards and wind instruments, and Chris Dennis as lead vocalist. Dennis is later replaced by Syd Barrett. Bob Klose lost intrest in the direction of the band and left before the group's first single was recorded.

1967

First album is released. The Piper At The Gates Of Dawn reached 6th on the UK charts, but makes it no higher than 131 in the US charts unfortunately. The album created attetion for band and the same year the Pink Floyd went on tour with Jimi Hendrix.

1968

With Syd Barrett's departure from the band due to becoming increasingly erratic, David Gilmour replaces Barrett and the band begins to move from psychedelic to progressive with the release of A Saucerful Of Secrets.

1973

The Dark Side Of The Moon one of the bands best known albums was realsed this year. To this date it is the bands most commercial sucecessful album to this date.

1975

Wish You Were Here the ninth studio album from Pink Floyd. Which was seen as a tribute to Syd who left 7 years before due to a his erratic behaviour. Shine On You Crazy Diamond and the song Wish You Were Here both relate to Syd's condition at the time.

1977

Animals the tenth studio album was set as a scathing critque of current poltical condtions at the time. The album art also started the trend of using a floating pig as a mascot for the band.

1979

The Wall in 1979 was set as new direction for the band the concept of the album was rock opera, autobigorphy linked with music as the main chracters were based on Syd Barrat and Rodger Waters. The album explores abandonment and isolation. The album was commercial sucess and inspired a film that went along with it. Another Brick In Wall a single on the album was also credited with ending use of corporal punishment in UK schools.

1985

Rodger Waters leaves the band, however the rest contiune without him. Rodger uses courts to try stop them, he loses and the they carry on without him.

1994

Division Bell the last Pink Floyd album for quite some time was realsed. It marked the start of a hiatus for the band.

2005

After a ten year hiatus, everybody inlcuing David Gilmour came back for the last live perfomance for the band this occured at the live aid benefit concert.

2006

One of the first members of Pink Floyd sadly passed away, Syd Barrart at the age of 60 dided from complications from his diabetes. He was attributed with wrting most of the bands first groundbreaking album The Piper at the Gates of Dawn, released in 1967.

2008

Rick Wright sadly passed away with cancer at the age of 65. He and Syd were the primary architects of the bands early sound.

2014

The Endless River the last album marked an end to new music from Pink Floyd the album did not prompt any accompanying tour as it was not seen as right without Rick Wright. The album uses previous recordings made with Wright during the recording of The Divsion Bell.
